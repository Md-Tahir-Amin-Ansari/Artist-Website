Meet Sunflower Artist

Experience the beauty of portraits

Sunflower Artist, the pen name of Shagufta Muzzammil, embodies the essence of vibrant and expressive art in Kolkata, IN. Specializing in oil pastel portraits, Shagufta captures intricate details and emotions that resonate deeply with viewers. Each piece tells a story, reflecting the beauty of human experience and connection. Explore her captivating portfolio, commission your own unique artwork, and join her on this artistic journey that celebrates creativity and expression.
